Wellness Quotient began with a simple, unfashionable conviction: that real health is built deliberately, over decades, in the company of someone who actually knows you.

We are not a clinic, and we are not a wellness app. We are a small practice and a private journal — for people who have the means to optimise every part of their lives and have decided that their health deserves the same attention. The writing here is evidence-led but never clinical, personal but never loud.
